BioBIKE is an on-line knowledge environment that puts in the hands of biologists without computing experience the resources that enable them to build new analytical tools for biological investigation. Click here to see a presentation of the graphical environment under development.

Modules are being developed using BioBIKE to lead students through interactive investigations of molecular biology. To run current versions of modules, you will need to have a BioBIKE window open and a window containing the module itself. Click here for refreshingly brief instructions on how to do this. The next generation of modules will make use of the graphical environment.

If you just want to get a basic idea of what the modules are about, click on any link below, but be aware that without an active BioBIKE window, you will not be able to see how the interaction inherent in the modules works.
