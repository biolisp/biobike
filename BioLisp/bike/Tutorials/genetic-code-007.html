BioBIKE gives you four tools with which to analyze the alien genetic code. Click on any of them to learn more:

MAKE-RANDOM-RNA 
Generates RNA according to your specifications

IN-VITRO-TRANSLATE
Translates given RNA according to alien genetic code

ANALYZE-AMINO-ACID-CONTENT
Breaks down translation products and reports number of amino acids found in resulting mixture

ANALYZE-PEPTIDE-CONTENT
Reports number of different peptides found in translation products

Use these tools to find out all you can about the alien genetic code:
- Is it a fixed-length code? Variable?
- Is it a 2-nucleotide code? 3-nucleotide? 4-nucleotide? More?
- What specific codons encode what specific amino acids?

Go to your local BioBIKE and find out!
