How to run a BioBIKE guided tour
  • Go to BioBIKE entry portal
        (note the link to documentation about BioBIKE)
     
  • Click on BioBIKE Public Cyanobacterial Edition (Virginia Commonwealth U.)
     
  • Make up a log in name (no spaces please) and type it in the LOGIN NAME box.
     
  • Enter your e-mail address if you like (if you do and something goes tragically wrong, you might get an e-mail with a solution to your problems).
     
  • Click New Login (Ignore the password. The site is open to all.)
     
  • Click the down arrow next to the Help box. Click Live Tutorials
     
  • Click any of the tutorials that suit your fancy.
