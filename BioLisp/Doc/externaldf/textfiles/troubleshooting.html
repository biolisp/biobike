Problems and Solutions

  • I can't log in - screen freezes
    • Problem: I tried to log in, entering a username. The screen says "Transferring data from biobike-8003.csbc.vcu.edu"... and that's all that happens.
    • Solution: It turned out that disabling a Skype plug-in permitted my laptop to access BioBIKE...

  • DISPLAY-MAP option of SEQUENCE-OF produces a blank screen
    COLORED option in ALIGNMENT-OF produces a blank screen
    • Problem: A new tab or window comes up but nothing appears in it.
    • Solution: Update your browser's Java add-on/plug-in. To update your Java add-on in Firefox:
      1. Tools --> Addons --> Plugins
      2. Click the small blue link at the top that says "Check to see if your plugins are up to date"
      3. Find Java, presumably something like Java (TM) Platform SE U31 6.0.310.5, and click the Update button and follow Java's installation instructions. You will probably have to completely restart Firefox for it to take effect.
