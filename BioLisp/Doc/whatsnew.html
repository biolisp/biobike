What's new with Biobike?


Sequence Viewer

Posted by the Biobike team Tuesday October 20, 2009

In the VPL, bring down Other-commands --> Sequence-Viewer

By selecting either an organism or a contig, you can easily view its entire sequence. Or you can select a From and/or a To to view only a certain segment. You can also use Search to find a particular pattern within the sequence.

Calc function

Posted by the Biobike team Monday October 12, 2009

If you're tired of using 'prefix' notation in the weblistener, we've got the perfect function for you. CALC easily handles the more common 'infix' notation.

For example in the weblistener you can now type in something like this:
    (calc "1-8+22")
and you'll get back
    > 15
