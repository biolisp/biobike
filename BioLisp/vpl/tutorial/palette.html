The Palette

OVERVIEW

This is the palette, positioned at the top of the screen:

Mousing over any of button displays a menu that offers access to a slice of BioBIKE functionality. The green buttons bring you to specific BioBIKE functions that can be brought down to your workspace. The blue buttons generally provide access to data.
